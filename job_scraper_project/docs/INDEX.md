## Quick Reference: Key Statistics

### Job Market
- **Average time to hire**: 42 days
- **Application-to-interview ratio**: 1:10 to 1:20
- **Hidden job market**: 70-80% never advertised
- **Referral success rate**: 40% higher than cold applications

### Resume & ATS
- **ATS rejection rate**: 75% before human review
- **Optimal resume length**: 1-2 pages
- **Keyword match target**: 40-60% of job description
- **Recruiter review time**: 6-8 seconds average

### AI Detection
- **Companies using AI detection**: 65%
- **Automatic rejection if flagged**: 43%
- **False positive rate**: 15-20%
- **Target AI detection score**: Below 40%

### Networking
- **Jobs filled through networking**: 85%
- **Networking response rate**: Typically 20-30%
- **LinkedIn importance**: 70%+ of recruiters use it
- **Referral hiring speed**: 50% faster

### Cover Letters
- **Employers preferring cover letters**: 56%
- **Impact of great cover letter**: 83% can overcome weak resume
- **Optimal length**: 250-400 words
- **Average read time**: 20-30 seconds
